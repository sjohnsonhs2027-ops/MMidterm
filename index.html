Overview

This community park in Harlem features an open lawn with terraced stone seating, a garden with perennial plants, and a paved plaza used for art installations, markets, festivals, and performances.

Open Lawn

Central green space for relaxation and gathering.

Terraced Seating

Stone seating that frames the landscape.

Garden

Perennial planting adds greenery and color.

Plaza

Supports markets, festivals, and performances.

Before & After Transformation

Before

Previously, Montefiore Square was smaller and less inviting, with limited seating and minimal greenery.

After

The redesigned square features expanded green space, terraced seating, improved lighting, and flexible public programming areas.

Community Impact

The Montefiore Park Neighborhood Association assists NYC Parks in maintaining the space, coordinating gardening, community involvement, and organizing events.
